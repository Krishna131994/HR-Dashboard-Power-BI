HR Analytics Dashboard — Power BI

An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI Desktop to help organizations 
understand and reduce employee attrition.

📊 Key Metrics Tracked
- Employee Count, Attrition Count & Attrition Rate (16.08%)
- Average Age, Income, Tenure, and Job Satisfaction Score

📈 Visuals Included
- Attrition by Job Role, Education, Salary Slab, and Age Group
- Attrition by Years at Company (trend line)
- Attrition by Job Satisfaction (matrix table)
- Marital Status distribution
- Department & Gender filters (HR / R&D / Sales | Male / Female)

🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Power Query (M Language)
- Dataset: HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset

### 💡 Key Insights
- 68.5% of attrition occurs in the lowest salary band (below 5K)
- Peak attrition at Year 1 of tenure — early engagement is critical
- Sales department has the highest attrition rate at ~20.6%
